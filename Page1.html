José Rizal

(June 19, 1861 – December 30, 1896)
José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda

was a Filipino nationalist, writer and polymath during the tail end of the Spanish colonial period of the Philippines. He is considered as the national hero (pambansang bayani) of the Philippines. An ophthalmologist by profession, Rizal became a writer and a key member of the Filipino Propaganda Movement, which advocated political reforms for the colony under Spain.
